How do I become a Citizen?

Go to the Citizen tab on the home screen, fill in your details and pay R150. You'll receive your Digital ID within 3–5 business days.

How do I submit a project?

Citizens submit projects free of charge. Non-citizens pay a R690 development fee. Go to the Submit tab on the home screen.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Capitec Pay, Visa/Mastercard, and EFT bank transfers. All payments are secure and receipts are issued.

How long does investor verification take?

Investor applications are reviewed by our admin team within 5 business days. You'll receive an email with your verification status.

Can I register from outside South Africa?

Yes. Citizens can register from any country using their passport number. International payments are supported.

What happens after I enrol in the Academy?

You'll receive an email with your course schedule, venue (if in-person) or online access link. Certificates are issued on completion.
